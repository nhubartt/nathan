90/90 hip stretch
2 min
Both legs bent at 90°, one in front, one behind. Hold each side 45 sec. Best hip mobility move for runners.
World's greatest stretch
1.5 min
Lunge forward, drop back knee, rotate upper body toward front leg, arm reaching up. 5 reps each side.
Thoracic spine rotation
1 min
Hands behind head, rotate upper back left and right slowly. Counteracts desk posture. Helps the left shoulder.
Hip flexor stretch (kneeling)
1.5 min
Kneeling lunge, drive hips forward. Hold 45 sec each side. Desk work shortens these — hurts your running gait.
Ankle circles + calf stretch
1 min

Cat-cow
1 min

Dead bug
2 min
